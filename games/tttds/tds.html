A new prototype I've been working on. It's still very much a work in progress, with it missing animation, some proper sprites, a proper background, working score, proper advancement and difficulty scaling, optimization, and quite a bit more...
Move with WASD. Click to shoot. Hit Space to go back in time (merges the timeline with 15 seconds ago...could bring back your past enemies, or double your current enemies! Also doubles you). If you have picked up a turret, hit E to place it. If you have picked up a trap (blue box), hit Q to place it.
This game is written in Actionscript, making use of Flashpunk.
