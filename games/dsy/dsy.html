Don't Shoot Yourself!
Don't Shoot Yourself! is an experiment I did to see if people would enjoy a game where you could control your own movements, but the other player controlled when you shot. In its current form, it's no more than a prototype reminiscent of any sort of basic shoot back and forth game. Contrary to the title, the idea is to try and destroy yourself.
Player 1 Controls:
- Move: W and S
- Shoot: Spacebar

Player 2 Controls:
- Move: Numpad 8 and 5
- Shoot: Numpad 0
I apologize in advance if the controls are a bit weird. I wasn't sure how to set two people up on the same keyboard without getting in each other's way. :)
