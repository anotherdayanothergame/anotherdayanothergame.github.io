How to play:
Hit your snail to give him a temporary speed boost. Be sure to watch fear and stamina. If its fear goes over 100 or its stamina hits 0, you lose!

Controls:
Arrow keys - D-pad.
Z - A.
X - B.
Space - Start.

Modes:
Single - vs AI.
Multi/Time Trial - vs self to submit time to leaderboards.
Multi/Other - vs a ghost of one of the top 3 players' best times for cash, the leaderboard spot, and bragging rights.
