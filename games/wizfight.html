WizFight

A game about dueling wizards and not really caring that all you can do is freeze the level while that other guy can draw lightning bolts from the sky by Matt Chelen, Katarina Klick, and Jordan Oakley.

Current Features:

* High speed deathmatch gameplay.
* Over 25 unique wizards, each with their own unique power.
* Four different gameplay modes, including classic deathmatch and king of the hill.
* Six arenas.
* Support for Xbox 360 gamepads.
* Support for custom levels, each with their own tilesets and music.

Planned Features:

* More wizards, more arenas, and more traps.
* More gameplay modes, including a mode with a stock amount of lives and team modes.
* A single-player mode.
* An integrated level editor.

The WizFight Alpha is available now for $3!
